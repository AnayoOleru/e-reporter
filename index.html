Why do I need to report this issues?

Air pollution can cause long-term damage to people's nerves, brain, kidneys, liver, and other organs. It's the number one cause of the prevalent diseases we have today, such as cancer. Water pollution kills organisms that depend on these water bodies. Dead fish, crabs, birds and sea gulls, dolphins, and many other animals often wind up on beaches. People can get diseases such as hepatitis, Cholera by eating seafood or drinking water that has been poisoned. Land pollution places humans and animals at risk, soil contamination on human health include breathing disorders, birth defects, and skin diseases. If you can report one or many of these issues you see on your community, you are helping to save the life of millions of people.
